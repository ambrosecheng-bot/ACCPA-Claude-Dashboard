⏳ Phase 3 starts after Phase 1 and 2 are embedded.

Chasing leads before delivery is fixed only amplifies the bottleneck. Phase 3 is deliberately last — once efficiency is proven and management visibility is in place, new capacity can be filled with higher-value clients.

Why Phase 3 is last — and why that matters
Fix delivery first

More clients on a strained system creates more chaos. Phase 1 frees capacity so new clients can actually be served well.

See the capacity

Phase 2's WIP dashboard reveals how much real capacity exists before we commit to acquiring more work.

Acquire with purpose

Target higher-value clients and cross-sell advisory to the existing 200 — not just more volume at the same unit price.

Phase 3 initiatives
💎
Offer design (Grand Slam)

Redesign the service offer to bundle compliance + advisory with clear outcomes and risk-reversal. Raise average client value — don't just add client count.

📖
StoryBrand messaging

Rewrite website and proposal copy: the client is the hero; we are the guide. Message: "We make IRD and AFRC invisible background noise so you can focus on your business."

🔄
Referral system

Systematise referrals from the existing 200 clients. Claude drafts outreach, case study content and referral thank-you notes.

📈
Cross-sell to existing clients

Offshore profits tax, AML/CTF advisory, restructuring — Claude drafts proposals for the highest-LTV opportunities in the current book.

🗂️
Lightweight CRM pipeline

Build a referral pipeline tracker (intake → CDD → proposal → won/lost). Claude converts meetings into CRM updates and drafts follow-up emails.

🤖
AI chatbot for lead capture

Odoo Website + Live Chat chatbot (Phase 2 groundwork) converts to a 24/7 lead-capture and triage tool that feeds the CRM pipeline.
